Is the patient black?*



*This difference in treatment was noted in the recommendations of the JNC 8. However, there is conflicting evidence on whether or not racial background determines the type of antihypertensive pharmacological intervention or if there are other variables that account for this difference:

  • "A Review of ACE Inhibitors and ARBs in Black Patients With Hypertension" — appears to support the notion that there may be a difference in pharmacological treatment
  • "Renin-Angiotensin System Inhibitors vs Other Antihypertensives in Hypertensive Blacks: A Meta-Analysis" — does not appear to support the notion that there may be a difference in pharmacological treatment
